Xavi's Small Books Library

ABOUT

This website serves as a visual catalogue of various zines, exhibition ephemeras, chapbooks, and other forms of small books in my home library. Unlike commercially published books with formal identification, these books often go under the radar of library systems, have small print runs, low-budgets, and can easily get lost. Because of this dilemma, I thought it’d be nice to nest them on a website, which would allow anyone to borrow or research them by inquiry via email or text.
